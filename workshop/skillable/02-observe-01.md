# Open Playground

While your Codespace builds, switch to the **Foundry Portal** tab (Tab 2) and
meet your hosted agent.

1. [] In the Foundry Portal, open **Agents** from the left navigation.
1. [] Select the **Zava Travel Concierge** agent to open it.
1. [] Select **Playground** (or **Try in playground**) to open an interactive
   chat with the agent.
